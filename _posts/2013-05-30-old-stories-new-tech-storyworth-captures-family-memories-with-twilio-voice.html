The phrase “ship it” means one thing to Nick Baum (pictured right), and another to his father.To Nick, those are last words you hear before deploying code.To his father, “ship it” means to put something on a boat and send it off.This generational gap intrigued Nick Baum and led him to found StoryWorth, a new way to share and record family stories.StoryWorth lets users create an online biography of their family’s history with archived pictures, text, and voice recordings.
