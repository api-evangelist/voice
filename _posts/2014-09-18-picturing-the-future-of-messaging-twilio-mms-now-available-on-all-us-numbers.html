Today, we’re happy to announce newly expanded Twilio messaging capabilities.For the first time ever, you can now send and receive MMS on all US Twilio phone numbers.We’ve eliminated months-long wait time and prohibitive costs typically associated with enabling MMS from your app by working with every carrier directly.Now with the click of a button you have three ways to communicate.You can use any Twilio number to send media, send SMS, or make a call.
