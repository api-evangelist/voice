Businesses all over the world are connecting with customers via text.Whether those messages are going across an ocean or around the corner, quality counts.Consistency and confidence in message deliverability enable you to deliver a high quality experience for the customer.Today, we’re happy to introduce you to the Messaging Quality Dashboard, to give you deeper insight into the messages you send everyday through Twilio.The Quality Dashboard is a one-stop-shop to see what happens to your messages when they’re sent through carriers.
