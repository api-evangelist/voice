In a surprise announcement, speech recognition startup Wit.ai has been acquired by Facebook today for an undisclosed sum.Developers use Wit.ai to get structured data from speech; it’s as simple as sending the speech to the service and it makes available structured JSON for your app to use.It is an incredible acceleration in the execution of our vision.
