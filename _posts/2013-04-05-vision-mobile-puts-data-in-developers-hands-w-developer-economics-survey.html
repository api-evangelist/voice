For developers, data is important but quality data is essential to make strategic decisions and build awesome products.We’re happy to work with Vision Mobile to help drive the latest Developer Economics survey, giving developers the information they need to build successful businesses.This ten minute survey compiles information on your type of work you’re doing, the size of your team and what you’re building.Your input is invaluable to the report.The more information collected, the higher quality of data to give back to developers.
