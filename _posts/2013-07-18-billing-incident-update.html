At 3:28am PDT/10:28am GMT, our monitoring systems reported an anomaly in our billing systems, which resulted in erroneous credit card charges and in some cases account suspensions.This incident affected 1.1% of customer accounts.The on call team immediately began an incident response, using the Twilio status dashboard at status.twilio.
