Cloud Elements gives developers an simple platform to organize all the different awesome cloud services they use everyday from GitHub, to Twilio, to PayPal and SendGrid.Today, Cloud Elements announced their Messaging Hub Platform, which makes it easier for developers to connect services they already use so they can spend less time managing tools and more time building.Their new Messaging Hub Platform integrates SendGrid’s email services with Twilio SMS so you can trigger custom email or SMS notifications, and monitor campaigns in real time.Let’s say you’re sending critical information from your app via email.You can use the Messaging Hub platform to trigger an SMS confirmation when that user receives the email.
